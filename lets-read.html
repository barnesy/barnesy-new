Let's Read

Award winning, Apple Appstore Featured. Family network for storytime connecting generations through the joy of reading together.

The Concept

I designed and prototyped a kids-book reading network. This prototype turned into a full product helping families stay connected. The app's main feature was remote video narration of in-app books. It was featured on the Apple Appstore and won a Webby award.

Product Screens

Impact

The app enabled grandparents, parents, and loved ones to read bedtime stories to children regardless of distance. By combining video recording with interactive books, Let's Read created meaningful connections across generations and helped maintain family bonds through the simple act of reading together.
